The following project was a visual identity design proposal for a personal trainer/life coach. The client approached me for a design which would emit both strength and devotion, as her coaching style is very personal and quite strict. It would also have to appeal to her largely female audience. What you see here was the first draft.

The project was sadly abandoned before completion, because the client joined an organization of personal trainers, so there was no need for an individual visual identity any more.

The mark combines a shield (strength) and a heart (compassion) into a single visually enticing element, especially when combined with colour. A bright colour scheme, featuring green as the dominant colour and red as the accent, is used to easily call attention to where we want it.

A simple one page website was mocked up, to show how the brand behaves in action. It features an introduction to who the trainer is, what she does, how she achieves results and an easy way to get in touch with her.
